    General Ledger System

    Advantages:

    • Helps in preparing and generating key financial reports.
    • Records transactions per account type for bookkeeping purposes.
    • Simplifies the audit of accounting records and/or the analysis of records.
    • Creates a complete list of financial transactions including assets, liabilities, equities, revenues, expenses, gains, and losses.
    • Features data protection functionalities to ensure the security of the system.
