About Us

What makes a company great? That’s simple.
It’s all about the people.

We are Your Partner in Success

MicroSource, Inc. is a Philippine-based software development and business process outsourcing company. We provide a wide range of software, hardware and IT consulting services including custom application development, network and telecommunications solutions, application support and maintenance, configuration management, and business intelligence solutions. Our team of professional engineers is committed to providing top-notch support to achieve 100% customer satisfaction at all times.

In building market acceptance and attaining a positive reputation in various local and international industries, we operate with a value proposition of timely and practical solutions at competitive rates. We use state-of-the-art equipment and ensure that our expert team of software engineers adapt well to technological advancements to address the needs of our customers.

We cater to all consumers, from startups, small- to medium-sized businesses, up to multinational corporations. We focus on proactive, market seeking organizations that are looking for opportunities to lessen their expenses, but maintaining the stability and development of their business technology operations.

Our team is dedicated to helping our clients reach their goals by providing efficient and effective IT systems that will assist in the realization of their business objectives.

Our Vision
